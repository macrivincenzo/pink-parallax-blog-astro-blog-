Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average SaaS company valuation in 2025?

SaaS valuations typically range from $500K to $50M+, with most companies valued between $2M and $10M. The exact value depends on ARR, growth rate, market category, and competitive position.

How is SaaS valuation different from traditional businesses?

SaaS companies have unique factors including recurring revenue models, scalability, network effects, and technology moats. Valuations focus on ARR, growth rates, and retention metrics rather than traditional profit multiples.

What's the difference between revenue multiples and ARR multiples for SaaS?

Revenue multiples include all revenue sources, while ARR multiples focus specifically on recurring revenue. ARR multiples are more accurate for SaaS businesses as they better reflect the predictable, scalable nature of software subscriptions.
