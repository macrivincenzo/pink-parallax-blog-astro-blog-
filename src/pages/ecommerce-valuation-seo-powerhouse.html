Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average e-commerce business valuation in 2025?

E-commerce valuations typically range from $100K to $10M+, with most businesses valued between $300K and $2M. The exact value depends on niche, revenue model, growth trajectory, and market position.

How is e-commerce valuation different from traditional businesses?

E-commerce businesses have unique factors including platform dependency, digital assets, scalability, automation potential, and online market dynamics. Valuations must account for these digital-specific elements.

What's the difference between SDE and EBITDA for e-commerce?

SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings) includes owner compensation and personal expenses, making it more accurate for small e-commerce businesses. EBITDA excludes these items and is better for larger, institutional online businesses.
