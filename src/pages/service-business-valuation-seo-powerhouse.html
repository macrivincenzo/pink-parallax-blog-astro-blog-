Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average service business valuation in 2025?

Service business valuations typically range from $200K to $8M+, with most businesses valued between $500K and $2M. The exact value depends on industry, client base, recurring revenue, and operational scalability.

How is service business valuation different from other businesses?

Service businesses have unique factors including client relationships, intellectual property, team dependency, and scalability constraints. Valuations must account for these service-specific elements and the importance of recurring revenue.

What's the difference between SDE and EBITDA for service businesses?

SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings) includes owner compensation and personal expenses, making it more accurate for small service businesses. EBITDA excludes these items and is better for larger, institutional service companies.
