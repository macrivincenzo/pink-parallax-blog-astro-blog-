Restaurant Valuation Calculator: Key Metrics

1. Seller's Discretionary Earnings (SDE) Analysis

SDE is the most accurate method for restaurant valuation. It calculates your true earning potential:

SDE Formula:

SDE = Net Income + Owner's Salary + Benefits + Depreciation + Interest + Non-recurring Expenses + Personal Expenses

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average restaurant valuation in 2025?

Restaurant valuations typically range from $200K to $5M+, with most restaurants valued between $400K and $1.2M. The exact value depends on concept, location, financial performance, and market conditions.

How is restaurant valuation different from other businesses?

Restaurants have unique factors including food costs, labor intensity, seasonality, location dependency, and brand reputation. Valuations must account for these hospitality-specific elements.

What's the difference between SDE and EBITDA for restaurants?

SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings) includes owner compensation and personal expenses, making it more accurate for small restaurants. EBITDA excludes these items and is better for larger, institutional restaurants.
