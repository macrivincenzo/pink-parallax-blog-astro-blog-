RISK MITIGATION
===============

Avoid These Mistakes:
- Don't buy backlinks from Fiverr or similar sites
- Avoid private blog networks (PBNs)
- Don't use excessive reciprocal linking
- Avoid automated software for link building
- Don't ignore email deliverability
- Avoid irrelevant or low-quality sites

Best Practices:
- Focus on high-quality, relevant backlinks
- Build genuine relationships with webmasters
- Create valuable, link-worthy content
- Use natural anchor text distribution
- Monitor backlink profile regularly
- Disavow toxic backlinks when necessary
